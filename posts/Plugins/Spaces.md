
The Spaces plugin lets you save and load "spaces". Each space includes pane layout, sidebar state (expanded or collapsed), and file open states.

![[Pasted image 19.png]]

### Save a space

Either click on the space icon in the ribbon to open "Manage spaces".

Give the current layout a new name, click on "Save", and it will appear in the list below.

### Load a space

To load or restore a space, either open "Manage spaces" and click on the "Load" button next to the space you wish to load, or use the "Load spaces" command to bring up a command-palette-like interface to search and choose spaces.


### Commands

This plugin has two commands that you can access through [[Command palette]]: manage spaces and load spaces. You can also define hotkeys for them.
